This repository contains the source file (Restaurant detail dashboard.pbix) for a detailed and interactive dashboard built in Microsoft Power BI Desktop. # Dashboard Purpose
* The primary goal is to transform raw restaurant transactional and operational data into clear, actionable insights.
* It serves as a centralized hub for monitoring the day-to-day health of the business and facilitating strategic decisions.
*  It helps management track key metrics essential for optimizing service, staffing, and menu pricing.

# Key Features and Expected Analysis
Based on the file name and standard restaurant reporting practices, the dashboard likely features analysis across the following areas:
* Sales and Revenue Analysis: 
  * Tracking daily, weekly, or monthly total revenue and average check size.
  * Breakdowns of sales by shift (e.g., Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner).
  * Performance comparison against targets or historical periods (Year-over-Year, Month-over-Month).
* Operational Metrics:
  * Analysis of covers (number of guests served) and table turnover rate.
  * Wait time and service time efficiency metrics.
* Menu and Product Performance:
  * Identification of best-selling and least-selling menu items.
  * Analysis of profitability per item or category to support menu engineering.
*  Staffing and Labor Analysis:
  * Visualization of labor costs as a percentage of revenue.
  * Tracking of employee efficiency or productivity metrics (e.g., sales per staff hour).
* Customer Insights:
  * Metrics related to customer volume, loyalty, or feedback (if integrated).
